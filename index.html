Our Services

Phone and Tablet Repairs

We repair all types of phones and tablets, including Androids, Samsung Tablets, and more. Also a soldering service for short circuitry and electronics

PC Building and Troubleshooting

We build custom PCs and offer expert troubleshooting services for both Windows and Linux systems. We can offer any specification from office workhorses to gaming behemoths, let us know what you need and we build to spec.

Networking Solutions

From home networks to enterprise solutions, we provide robust networking infrastructure and support. Also can provide cybersecurity checks and reassure you when you do your thing online.

Console repairs

We can repair and support many consoles. We can restore the old and the new, to good condition, even the retro stuff. Have you seen the inside of an old Atari, it's like two cables....'

3D Printing

3D Printing facilities are available, ready to help print bespoke parts as a fix in some cases. A limited Print to order service might be coming soon.

Software Support

We offer technical support for a wide range of software, including Microsoft Office, Libre Office, most Windows and Linux systems.
